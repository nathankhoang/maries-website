Our Mission

Welcome to Au.D. Viet Translations

Whether you're an audiologist or a student, these resources are designed to help you bridge the language gap and communicate more effectively with Vietnamese-speaking patients. Whether you need translated materials for patient instructions, assessments, or testing materials, we are here to support you.

We understand the challenges of navigating both language and hearing barriers in audiology. That's why we are dedicated to providing accurate translations, ensuring that every patient receives clear and effective communication.

Let us help you better serve the Vietnamese community. Together, we can make audiology more inclusive and accessible.

Pronouns & Greetings

Testing Instructions

Step-by-step instructions for otoscopy, tympanometry, pure tone audiometry, SRT, WRS, and masking procedures.
